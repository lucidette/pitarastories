About Pitara

Pitara is curating a magic box of public-domain Indian digital treasures. We find beloved Indian stories and try to present them online beautifully. We also want to use new digital tools and visualisations to tell uniquely Indian stories.

Pitara is purely a labour of love and we welcome contributions. Please write to us at hello at pitara dot com
